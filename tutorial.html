Welcome to SpellForge!

Aren't sure how this works? You've come to the right place!

In order to create a spell build, click the spell build button and select 'new build'. This will allow you to name a build, select its class, and add spells to it.

Happy Building!

Adding A Spell

Once inside your build, select your class. once a class has been selected, a list of available spells will appear. Drag the name of the spell you want into the spell build section Once the spell is added, its material components will be added to the list beside it.


Removing A Spell

If you decide you don't want a spell in your build anymore, dont fret! Once a spell is inside your build, drag it back into the available spells section to remove it. This will also remove its materials from the material components list.
